Fetchivo AI Privacy Policy

  (Optimized with reference to mainstream AI download tools)

  Last Updated: March 26, 2026

  1. Introduction

  Welcome to Fetchivo AI (the "App"), a tool that provides AI-powered search and content download services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App, in compliance with Google Play Console requirements and applicable data protection laws.

Important Notice: This App is for personal legal use only. Downloading copyrighted content must comply with the original platform’s terms of service and local laws and regulations.

  2. Information We Collect

  We follow the principle of "minimum necessary" and collect only data essential to providing core services:

  • Data you provide voluntarily:
  •        
    • Download URLs you enter
    • Search queries you submit for AI functions
  • Automatically collected device information:
  •        
    • Device model, operating system version, screen resolution
    • App version, interaction data (for UI and performance optimization)
    • Network status (to stabilize download and AI services)

  We do not collect sensitive personal data such as location, contacts, call logs, or internal storage content beyond the minimum scope required to process download requests.

  3. How We Use Your Data

  Your data is used only for the following core purposes:

  • To process your download requests, interact with target content platforms to retrieve and deliver the content you request
  • To call the Google Gemini API to generate AI suggestions and search results based on your queries
  • To optimize App performance, fix bugs, improve user experience and service stability
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to valid legal requests (e.g., court orders, regulatory inquiries)

  We will not use your data for targeted advertising, marketing push, user profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App’s core functions.

  4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  We share your data only in the following limited situations:

  • Third-party service providers:
  •        
    • Google Gemini API: Your search queries are transmitted to Google Gemini to support AI functions, governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.
    • Content platforms: Your entered URLs are shared with corresponding video or content platforms to retrieve and process the content you request to download.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your data if required by laws, regulations, or valid legal requests (e.g., to protect the rights or safety of us or others).
  • No commercial sharing: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

  5. Data Retention and Deletion

  The App does not store or upload any of your personal data, search content, download links, or history records to our servers. All data generated during your use is saved locally only on your device and will never be transmitted to or retained by our backend systems.

  You may delete all local data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app’s cache in your device settings;

  6. User Rights

  As a user of our App, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you by contacting us (see Section 12).
  • Right to deletion: You may delete all local data at any time by clearing the app’s cache in your device settings.
  • Right to object: You may stop using the App and clear the app’s cache to object to our processing of your data.

  Please note that due to App design limitations and the fact that no user data is stored on our servers, we currently do not support data correction, export, or server-side deletion functions.

  7. Data Security

  We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction, including:

  • Transmission encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers (including interactions with Google Gemini API and content platforms) is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Local data protection: Data stored locally on your device is protected by the device’s built-in security mechanisms, and we do not access or control local data without your explicit operation.
  • Access control: We authorize only necessary personnel to access data related to App operation (excluding user local data), who are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Regular audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks related to App operation and data transmission.

  While we take reasonable steps to ensure data security, no Internet transmission or electronic storage method is absolutely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of local data on your device (which is primarily protected by your device’s security settings).

  8. Children’s Privacy

  The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13 (via data transmitted to third-party services), we will immediately delete such data from relevant systems.

  9. International Data Transfers

  Data transmitted to third-party service providers (such as Google Gemini API) may be processed and stored on servers outside your country of residence, including the United States and other regions where our third-party service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of such data to these jurisdictions.
